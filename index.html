About Us

Build Scape Limited is a Civil Engineering and Building Construction firm undertaking projects generally in Nairobi.

The company was founded in 2014 by Mr.Maurice Mwangi Mwagiru & Mr. John Kamau Ngugi. They are both very experienced members of the construction industry having been in the industry for an accumulated period of over 15 years and having engaged in numerous structures in Nairobi.

The Young Build Scape limited is currently undertaking both building and civil works as Main Contractors for small sized projects
